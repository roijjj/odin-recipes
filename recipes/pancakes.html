Fluffy Pancakes

Home

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and perfect for a weekend breakfast!

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 cup milk
  6. 1 egg
  7. 2 tablespoons melted butter
  8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
