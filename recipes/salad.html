Fresh Garden Salad

Home

This salad is a refreshing mix of greens, vegetables, and a light vinaigrette dressing!

Ingredients

  1. 4 cups mixed salad greens
  2. 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  3. 1 cucumber, sliced
  4. 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  5. 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  6. 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  7. 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  8. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  9. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  10. Salt and pepper to taste
