This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  1. 12 lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound ground beef
  3. 1 onion, chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  6. 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  7. 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  8. 1 teaspoon salt
  9. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  10. 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  11. 1 egg
  12. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  13. 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
